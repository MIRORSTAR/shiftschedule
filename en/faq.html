Answers to the most common questions about Shift Schedule

Schedule

The second schedule is a separate work schedule layered on top of your main one. Useful if you sometimes work a second job on its own shift cycle.


How to enable: tap any rest day → Second schedule → configure. Set the cycle start date and the number of work/rest days. On days when the second schedule is active, a briefcase icon appears in the calendar cell.


Note: the second schedule supplements the main one, it doesn't replace it. If both are active on the same day, the main status is shown in the cell while the briefcase icon signals the second job. Your partner will see that you're working — the shared day off won't be counted for that day.

Alarms

This is usually caused by battery settings on your phone — especially on Xiaomi, Samsung and Huawei. Three steps:


1. Autostart (Xiaomi/MIUI): Settings → Apps → ShiftSchedule → Autostart → enable. Without this the alarm won't wake up if the app was closed.


2. Battery: Settings → Battery → Apps → ShiftSchedule → select No restrictions.


3. Android 12+: the system may require exact alarm permission — go to Settings → Apps → ShiftSchedule → Alarms & reminders → allow.


If you've done all this and alarms still don't fire — email us at mirorstar@yandex.ru with your phone model and Android version.



This is standard Android behavior: all scheduled alarms are deleted by the system on reboot. Shift Schedule can restore them — but it needs to be opened after the phone boots.


So you don't forget: after a reboot, a quiet reminder notification from the app will appear in your notification shade. Tap it — Shift Schedule will open and automatically reschedule alarms for upcoming days.


On Xiaomi/MIUI devices: enable Autostart for Shift Schedule in phone settings (Settings → Apps → ShiftSchedule → Autostart). This allows the app to restore alarms right after reboot without any action from you.

The bell icon only appears next to work shifts in the day list — not next to days off. Make sure your anchor date is set correctly and upcoming days have Work status. If the status is correct but there's no icon — try tapping Set alarms again.

PRO version

Sync

PRO is required. Create an account in Settings → Sync, then sign in on both devices with the same credentials. The schedule syncs automatically every 3 minutes after changes.

PRO required. Ask your colleague to share their 6-character code from Settings → Sync → Partner code. Enter it on your device in the same section — their schedule will appear on your calendar in a different color.

Weather

Two conditions for weather to work:


1. Location permission. Phone Settings → Apps → Shift Schedule → Permissions → Location → allow. Without this the app doesn't know which city to load the forecast for.


2. Internet access. The forecast is loaded from the network — without internet the weather won't appear. If your connection was off, try refreshing or restarting the app when connected.


If both conditions are met — include your app version and write to us.

Without an account your schedule is stored only on your phone. If your phone breaks, gets lost, or you reinstall the app — all data will be lost with no way to recover it.


An account (free with PRO) gives you:

  • Restore when switching phones
  • Sync between devices

To create an account: Settings → Sync → Create account.
